What is an Oracle?

Like to Orate, to speak of knowledge of something. Something with answers to questions and queries. Oracles may often hold a mystical association for many people. The ability of something outside of ourselves to answer questions that come from within ourselves is a spiritual concept that each person carries different belief structures about. Some point to God, some point to Tao, some don't point, some point to universal scientific laws. Whether it is chance or it is destiny, it changes, and changes continue to changlessly change.

If we can begin to look at oracles as a tool to get to know oneself, we can begin to find answers for the questions we have. Oracles are found and created when we focus on questions and start listening to the truth inside ourselves.

The Lord whose oracle is at Delphi neither reveals nor conceals, but gives a sign - Heraclitus
Beauty is the oracle that speaks to us all - Luis Barragan
Heed these words, you who wish to probe the depths of nature. If you do not find within yourself that which you seek, neither will you find it outside yourself. If you ignore the wonders of your own house, how do you expect to find other wonders? in you is hidden the treasure of treasures. Know thyself, and you will know the universe and the gods. - Inscribed at the Oracle of Delphi in Greece
